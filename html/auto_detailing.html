Advanced Paint Correction & Protection

Pricing: Starting at $600+

Our most advanced auto paint restoration and protection procedure. This package offers an intensive 2+ stage paint correction through wet sanding, machine compounding, buffing and polishing in order to remove as many defects as possible. The surfaces are then protected by an industry leading Nanodiamond coating. There are two different packages available, Diamond Body 18 month or Diamond Body 36 month coatings. The result will be a show car finish that will last with the proper care, no waxing required.

Full Professional Detail

Pricing: Starting at $450+

Hand washed and clay barred to remove debris and contaminants that have bonded to the paint. Includes a single step machine polish process that removes light scratches and scuffs leaving a shiny and rich look to the exterior. The exterior is then top coated with wax and sealant to preserve the finish. The interior will receive steam cleaning/shampooing of the carpets and upholstery, removing stains and odors and leaving it virtually dry upon completion. All plastics, vinyl and leather will be cleaned and conditioned with a sealant protectant that will prevent fading and cracking and create a hydrophobic surface. Finally, all glass is cleaned to a crystal clear finish and topped with a hydrophobic coating.
