Our Auto Detailing Process

Exterior Detailing

Step 1 - Remove all leaves and debris from cowl and trunk jamb

Step 2 - Clean each tire, wheel, inner fender well, jamb, and underhood and engine with degreaser and pressure washer

Step 3 - Soap and scrub all exterior areas of the vehicle as well as all floor mats

Step 4 - Clay, rinse and dry all exterior areas of the vehicle

Interior Detailing

Step 1 - Remove all debris from interior of vehicle

Step 2 - Pretreat, agitate, extract and steam all carpet and cloth surfaces

Step 3 - Clean and dress all plastic, leather and vinyl surfaces

Step 4 - Wipe down the visors and headliner fabric with ONR 256:1 to clean and remove grime without staining or damaging the fabric

Step 5 - Clean all interior glass

Step 6 - Final vacuum and dry
