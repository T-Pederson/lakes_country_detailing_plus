Scratch Repair

The next generation of glass scratch removal is here. With GlasWeld's Gforce Max tool, we achieve distortion-free scratch removal. Our professional technique doesn't remove glass from the surface. Instead, we use heat, friction, and compound to level out the damage. Think of it like ironing wrinkles out of clothes.
